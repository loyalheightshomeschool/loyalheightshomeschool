Animals of the Puget Sound

Instructor: Stephanie Burkemoore
Ages: 9 - 18
Maximum class size: 10
Explore the rich animal world beneath Pacific Northwest waters. Students will learn how Puget Sound animals survive, adapt, and interact within their ecosystem. Whether they already love marine life or are just starting out, this class will spark curiosity about our environment.

Art Adventure

Instructor: Stefanie Kahler
Ages: 4 - 8
Maximum class size: 10
Let’s have an adventure exploring different styles of art and art media. Priority will be placed on process over product and on meeting each child where they are in their personal and artistic development. I look forward to making art together!

Art Appreciation

Instructor: Sara Cammeresi
Ages: 8 - 18
Maximum class size: 10
Learn how to ‘read’ visual art. This is not an art history course. We will not be discussing what scholars say about what makes this or that piece important. Rather, we will practice looking closely, noticing details, and finding our own personal connections to the art.

Art of Architecture

Instructor: Melissa Moore
Ages: 6 - 10
Maximum class size: 10
In this class, we will explore famous architects like Maya Lin and Frank Lloyd Wright through books and hands-on experiments like building memorials and castles.

Beginning Programming

Instructor: Ryan Kooger
Ages: 9 - 18
Maximum class size: 10
This beginner class introduces students to coding through playful, visual projects with the Snap! programming language. Participants will create animations, stories, and games while exploring sequencing, loops, events, and debugging.

Drawing

Instructor: Shannon Fleck
Ages: 10 - 18
Maximum class size: 10
In this playful drawing class, students build strong drawing skills and techniques while exploring past and present artists for inspiration. Students will experiment with different drawing styles while learning a little art history.

Intermediate Programming

Instructor: Ryan Kooger
Ages: 9 - 18
Maximum class size: 10
This intermediate class is intended for students who have experience with programming. Teacher approval required. Students will continue improving their understanding of coding through interactive projects using the JavaScript programming language.

Soccer

Instructor: Michael
Ages: 9 - 12
Maximum class size: 16
Build soccer skills, confidence, and teamwork in this fun class. Players will practice passing, dribbling, shooting, and defense through drills and scrimmages. All skill levels are welcome, with a focus on improvement, teamwork, and staying active.

Storybook Movement

Instructor: Miranda Veenhuysen
Ages: 4 - 8
Maximum class size: 12
In Storybook Movement, students engage in stories through motion. As a book is read aloud, children will act out characters and actions with their bodies, building imagination, listening skills, and creativity while staying active and engaged.
