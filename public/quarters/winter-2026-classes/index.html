Epics and Adventures

Instructor: Pacifica Writers’ Workshop
Ages: 6 - 12
Maximum class size: 14
$286
The perfect class for the young explorer in your family. In it, students will build intricate worlds and craft exciting tales. They will also play adventurous games and enjoy other swashbuckling activities to inspire their writing.

Human Anatomy

Instructor: Ellen Marceau
Ages: 6 - 11
Maximum class size: 10
$89
Explore the amazing human body in this fun, hands-on anatomy class! Learn about the different systems we have in our bodies through fun, hands-on activities. Perfect for curious scientists!

How to be a Person

Instructor: Stefanie Kahler
Ages: 12 - 18
Maximum class size: 10
$95
This fun, hands-on class empowers kids to become more confident, capable, and independent in their daily lives. Students will learn essential life skills through engaging activities, humor, and real-world practice. Skills like organization, addressing letters, and self-care will be discussed.

Creative Dance

Instructor: Miranda Veenhuysen
Ages: 4 - 8
Maximum class size: 10
$155
Burst, spin, float—fall in love with dance! Students will explore dance concepts in a joyful, supportive class using Anne Green Gilbert’s creative dance curriculum and Brain Dance warm-up. Wear comfy clothes and dance barefoot.

SUMM Mathterschool

Instructor: Seattle Universal Math Museum
Ages: 7 - 10
Maximum class size: 10
$313
Whether you’re a math geek or math curious, let’s get hands-on with some seriously cool math activities, including puzzles, art, and games. (www.loyalheightshomeschool.org)

Math and Logic Puzzles

Instructor: Sara Cammeresi
Ages: 10 - 13
Maximum class size: 10
$155
Explore the world of logic and puzzles in this fun, interactive class! Develop problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and creativity through challenging games, brainteasers, and riddles. Perfect for curious minds eager to think outside the box!

Intro to Acting

Instructor: Malinda Sorci
Ages: 8 - 14
Maximum class size: 10
$175
Explore the world of imagination and creativity! In this fun-filled class, kids learn basic acting skills through games, storytelling, and role-play, building confidence on stage and beyond.

Ecology of the Puget Sound

Instructor: Stephanie Burkemoore
Ages: 8 - 12
Maximum class size: 10
$175
Explore the plants, animals, and ecosystems of the Puget Lowlands in this fun, hands-on class for young learners. Students discover how nature works and how to care for the environment around them.

Teen Craft Circle

Bring your own craft and join our Teen Craft Circle! Whether you knit, draw, embroider, or bead, this is a fun, relaxed space to create, share ideas, and connect with friends. Please bring easy to tidy crafts.

GeoQuest

Instructor: Ashley Titus
Ages: 8 - 14
Maximum class size: 10
$125
In this geography and mapping class, students learn to read physical maps, analyze how geography influences industry and culture, and explore diverse landforms. The course culminates in a final project where students design their own country and map, showcasing its geography-driven industries and lifestyle.

The Game of Go

Instructor: Seattle Go Center (Drew Barnett)
Ages: 7 - 18
Maximum class size: 10
$95
Join us for lectures and game play aimed at teaching newer players the game of Go, an ancient Asian strategy game. We’ll start with small boards and simple instructions and eventually progress to a full territory-based game.
