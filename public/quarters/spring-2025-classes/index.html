Cosplay Costuming

Instructor: Shannon Hillinger
Ages: 12 - 18
Maximum class size: 10
$95
Cosplay is a blend word of “costume play.” In this class you’ll learn how to design and create costumes based off of popular characters or your own imagination! Learn planning, sewing, crafting, and makeup skills while building confidence and creativity in a supportive environment. (loyalheightshomeschool.org)

Science of the Senses

Instructor: Shannon Hillinger
Ages: 4 - 7
Maximum class size: 10
$95
Sight, sound, taste, touch, and scent all help us understand our world. But, do you know how and why we can do these things? Let’s all learn together about how we interact with our world, and some of our senses beyond those five, like your sense of balance! We will learn with hands-on activities, games, and more. (loyalheightshomeschool.org)

Nature Games

Instructor: Stephanie Burkemoore
Ages: 6 - 9
Maximum class size: 15
$165
Learn about our local ecology – the ways living and nonliving things interact. What is a watershed? How does symbiosis work? What happens to salmon when they set out to sea? We will play field games and board games to explore how our environment stays in balance. Parents welcome to join. Class held both outdoors and indoors. (loyalheightshomeschool.org)

Ages: 6 - 9
